Element s3m:DvTemporalType / dvtemporal-date
Namespace No namespace
Annotations
 
Represents top-open intervals of exactly one day in length on the timelines of dateTime, beginning on the beginning moment of each day, up to but not including the beginning moment of the next day). For non-timezoned values, the top-open intervals
disjointly cover the non-timezoned timeline, one per day. For timezoned values, the intervals begin at every minute and therefore overlap.

Element s3m:DvTemporalType / dvtemporal-duration
Namespace No namespace
Annotations
 
A datatype that represents durations of time. The concept of duration being captured is drawn from those of [ISO 8601], specifically durations without fixed endpoints. For example, "15 days" (whose most common lexical representation in duration is
"'P15D'") is a duration value; "15 days beginning 12 July 1995" and "15 days ending 12 July 1995" are not duration values. This datatype can provide addition and subtraction operations between duration values and between duration/dateTime value pairs, and can be the result of
subtracting dateTime values.
