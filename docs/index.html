Bio-Tradukisto

Bio-Tradukisto is a Semantic Web tool to allow a clinician or a biomedical researcher to communicate the meaning of the information recorded about their patients or research subjects, as well as to transfer validation capabilities to secondary data users in a way that is machine computable and transparent to those users. Bio-Tradukisto was developed in response to the National Institutes of Health Biomedical Data Translator Program.
